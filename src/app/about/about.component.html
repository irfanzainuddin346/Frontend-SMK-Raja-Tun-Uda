About




Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Raja Tun Uda originally occupied the premises of the Bayan Lepas English School. It was known as Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bayan Lepas. In 1976, when the Penang International Airport underwent a major expansion, the school was relocated to its present site along Jalan Tengah. This happened alongside Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Permai, which is today still its next door neighbour. The original site is now occupied by the Malaysia Airport Training Centre.
